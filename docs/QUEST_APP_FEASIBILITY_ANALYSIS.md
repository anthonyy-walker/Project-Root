## âš ï¸ Risks & Considerations

### Legal/Compliance Risks
1. **Age restrictions:** COPPA compliance for under-13 users
   - Need parental consent systems
   - No direct communication with minors
   - Strict data privacy controls

2. **Gambling concerns:** Prize-based competitions might be regulated
   - Skill-based vs. chance-based (your model is skill = OK)
   - May need legal review per jurisdiction
   - Terms of service must be airtight

3. **Epic Games ToS:** Ensure your app doesn't violate Epic policies
   - Can't promise V-Bucks you don't control
   - Must clearly state "not affiliated with Epic"
   - Risk of ban if Epic sees it as exploitative

### Operational Risks
1. **Cheating/Exploitation:** Players using hacks, bots, alt accounts
   - Need robust anti-cheat detection
   - Manual review for top winners
   - Ban systems

2. **Payment fraud:** Stolen credit cards, charge-backs
   - Use reputable payment processors
   - Fraud detection systems

3. **Customer support:** Handling disputes, prizes, tech issues
   - Need support team/system at scale

### Financial Risks
1. **Negative margins early:** Will lose money months 1-4
   - Need $2,000-5,000 runway capital
   
2. **Scaling costs:** Server, payment processing, support
   - AWS/cloud: $50-200/month at scale
   - Payment fees: 3-5% of prize pool
   - Support: $500-2,000/month if outsourced

3. **Epic payout delays:** Revenue paid 60-90 days after earning
   - Need cash flow management
   - Can't rely on immediate revenue
